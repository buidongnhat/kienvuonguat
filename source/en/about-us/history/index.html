History

Kien Vuong Co., Ltd – formerly Van Loi Co., Ltd. was established in 1991, the head office is located at Lot 22, Street 7, Tan Tao Industrial Park, Tan Tao A Ward, Binh Tan District, Ho Chi Minh City. - is one of the first private companies to be established under the corporate law since 1990.

More than many years, with all efforts, Kien Vuong has become the official distributor of many chemical and ingredients brands such as: Dow Chemical, Dupont, Solvay, Kuraray, ….. Coming to Kien Vuong, not only will clients be provided with quality products, but also, more importantly, they will receive the full value package: from prestige and quality assurance of goods from manufacturers, the legality, the process of preservation, storage and transportation which support customers to maintain product quality and productivity.

The company focuses on providing chemicals and ingredients for the following industries: pharmaceutical, cosmetics, food, aquaculture, seafood processing, animal feed, and industrial. Our branches and representative offices spread across Vietnam -- Hanoi, Bac Ninh, Da Nang, Nha Trang, Ho Chi Minh City and Can Tho -- to serve customers to the fullest.

With the mission statement: To bring "goods" from the World into Vietnam and bring "products" of Vietnam to the World through professional distribution channels, in order to harmonize the interests of the company, partners and communities, Kien Vuong always puts credibility, responsibility and professionalism to the forefront, constantly strives to strictly implement and fulfill our mission to bring trust to our customers.

1991 Established Van Loi Co.,Ltd
1996 Restructured to Huu Tin Co., Ltd
2000 Established Branch of Huu Tin Co. Ltd in Hanoi
2005 Established Representative Offices of Huu Tin Co., Ltd in Da Nang, Can Tho and Nha Trang
2008 Restructured into Kien Vuong Co., Ltd
2014 Established Kien Vuong Co., Ltd. Branch in Bac Ninh
2016 Established Huu Tin Pharmachem Co., Ltd in Long An
2020 Established Mekong Delta Branch of Kien Vuong Co., Ltd in Vinh Long
2023 Established Branch of Kien Vuong Co., Ltd. in Hung Yen
2026 Restructured Huu Tin Pharmachem Co., Ltd to Kien Vuong Tay Ninh Limited
