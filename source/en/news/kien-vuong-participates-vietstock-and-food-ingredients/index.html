Kien Vuong participates VIETSTOCK and FOOD INGREDIENTS

11/11/2022

Following the success of the International Exhibition on Coatings and Printing Inks (Coatings Vietnam 2022), Kien Vuong recently participated in 2 booths at the Exhibition of Livestock, Animal Feed and Meat Processing at the same time. in Vietnam (Vietstock 2022) and Vietnam International Exhibition of Ingredients, Food and Beverages (Food Ingredients2022)

Both exhibitions took place from October 12-14, 2022 at Saigon Exhibition and Convention Center (SECC), District 7, Ho Chi Minh City. Attracting thousands of visitors to visit the display booths of famous brands in the country and the world.

 

Vietstock 2022, hosted by the Department of Livestock Production (Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development), is a trading forum between exhibition partners and leading suppliers of products and solutions for the livestock industry. , animal feed, domestic, regional and international meat processing industry. At this event, customers are also connected with suppliers to grasp the latest technology. This year, there are not only exhibitions, but also seminars and discussions to provide in-depth knowledge as well as update the latest trends of the livestock industry.

For the first time at the exhibition, Kien Vuong brought solutions to improve the environment for livestock, poultry and aquaculture. At the same time, Kien Vuong promote the raw materials, excipients and auxiliary substances in the fields of livestock (livestock, poultry, aquatic products), veterinary medicine, aquatic products and animal feed production.

Food Ingredients Vietnam is the leading event for the food and beverage ingredients industry in the ASEAN region held every two years. This event is organized by Ho Chi Minh City Food Association (FFA), Vietnam Association of Food Science and Technology (VAFoST) and Informa Markets (Trading platform, initiative and development for specialized industries and markets. separate) organization coordination. Food Ingredients Vietnam is the leading event for the food and beverage ingredients industry in the ASEAN region held every two years. This event is organized by Ho Chi Minh City Food Association (FFA), Vietnam Association of Food Science and Technology (VAFoST) and Informa Markets (Trading platform, initiative and development for specialized industries and markets ).

Within the framework of Food Ingredients Vietnam, a series of technical and specialized seminars took place; Through this, visitors can update new trends in the food and beverage industry, find new sources of raw materials, and develop business cooperation relationships. The exhibition brings together more than 100 regional and global food and beverage exhibitors, giving customers the opportunity to effectively connect business and expand the market.

With the companion of Solvay Vietnam, Kien Vuong introduced at Food Ingredients Vietnam 2022 exhibition solutions to control microorganisms and bacteria in the food processing. Applications for all food industries such as meat, poultry, seafood, beverage, dairy, vegetables, fruits and vegetables, surface and equipment disinfection.

Through these two exhibitions, Kien Vuong had the opportunity to connect trade with key enterprises in the industry, update more knowledge and development trends of the market in the post-Covid-19 recovery period. Simultaneously approaching and understanding the needs of many customers, thereby drawing on experience and aiming to bring better efficiency to customers and partners.
