Kien Vuong holds seminar “ Platform solutions with focus on soft-gel and solubilization ”

08/02/2022

With the aim to create a bridge between suppliers and customers, on November 25, 2016, Kien Vuong Co., Ltd. held a seminar "PLATFORM SOLUTIONS WITH FOCUS ON SOFT-GEL AND SOLUBILIZATION" at the Hanoi University of Pharmacy (No. 15, Le Thanh Tong, Hoan Kiem District, Hanoi)

 

 

The seminar had the participation of speakers including Mr. Umakanth - BASF Technical Expert in the Asia Pacific region, and Dr. Tran Van Thanh - Lecturer in the Department of Pharmacology, Faculty of Pharmacy, Ho Chi Minh City University of Pharmacy. In addition, there arelecturers from Hanoi University of Pharmacy and nearly 40 guests from pharmaceutical and dietary supplement companies, who are also frequent customers of Kien Vuong for many years. .

 

Mr. Nguyen Tuan Cuong - Deputy General Director of Market Development of Kien Vuong Co., Ltd speaks at conference 

At the seminar, the guests listened to a report on experimental research results to improve the disintegration of Ginkgo softgel capsules from the University of Medicine and Pharmacy in Ho Chi Minh City. In addition, BASF technical expert Umakanth introduced teachers, lecturers as well as guests to the application platforms of BASF excipients in the preparation and in-depth discussion on solutions to improve the solubility of BASF. dissolve, increase drug bioavailability.

 

 Dr. Tran Van Thanh presented a report on experimental research results to improve the disintegration of Ginkgo softgel capsules.

 

Mr. Umakanth - BASF Technical Expert in the Asia Pacific region introduced the application platforms of BASF excipients in pharmaceutical formulations.

Through the seminar, Kien Vuong would want to build a connection between BASF and customers. Thereby increasing the value of Kien Vuong's brand with suppliers and above all, contributing to building the image and brand of Kien Vuong in the pharmaceutical industry in Vietnam. Not only that, this seminar is also a premise to open up new cooperation between units and departments (Hanoi University of Pharmacy, Research Institute, units operating in the field of pharmaceutical products, pharmaceutical production) and increase the value of BASF's products to a new position, promising to bring many breakthrough research projects in the future.

Mr. Umakanth answered questions from guests

 

This conference is also a premise to open new cooperation relationships with departments such as Hanoi University of Pharmacy, Research Institute, and units operating in the field of pharmaceutical production in Vietnam.

At the end of the seminar, Kien Vuong receives a positive evaluation from the supplier and a lot of attention from customers. From the reviews and questions asked by customers, Kien Vuong opens up many development directions in the coming time to better meet the needs and expectations of customers. Thereby, tightening the relationship between suppliers, Kien Vuong and customers.
