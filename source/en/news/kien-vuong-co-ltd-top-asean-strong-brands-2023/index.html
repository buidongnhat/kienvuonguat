Kien Vuong Co., Ltd – Top ASEAN Strong Brands 2023

10/05/2023

On the evening of April 22, 2023, the Asia Business Center in collaboration with the Vietnam Business Association in Malaysia (VNBIZ) held the 7th ASEAN - Vietnam Economic Cooperation Forum and the Ceremony to announce Top ASEAN Strong Brands, ASEAN Successful Leader 2023 and ASEAN Quality Products and Services 2023. The Forum and the Announcement Ceremony were held at JW Marriott Hotel Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia.

Attendees of the event include Mr. Le Gia Kien, Counselor, Embassy of Vietnam in Malaysia; Mr. Ngo Sy Tuyen, Chairman of VNBIZ; Ms. Nguyen Thanh Van, Vice President of VNBIZ; Dr. Bui Van Quyen, Vice President, and General Secretary of the Vietnam Invention Association; Mr. Tran Van Doanh, Director of Asia Business Development Research Center; Mr. Nguyen Nam, Deputy Director, Institute of Survey and Evaluation of Competitiveness Index. Representatives from Malaysian businesses and organizations include Mr. Andrew Goh Boon Kim, Vice President, Federation of Malaysian Manufacturers, National Chamber of Commerce, and Industry Malaysia (NCCIM); Mr. Hambali Mukhlas, Chairman of the International Business Committee of NCCIM; Mr. Ada Foo, Vice President of Malaysian Business Association and many other business representatives from Vietnam and Malaysia.

 

Mr. Le Gia Kien - Counselor, Embassy of Vietnam in Malaysia (far right of the photo) awarding the ASEAN 2023 Strong Brand Certificate to the businesses

 

Ms. Nguyen Thi Thanh Van - Vice President of Vietnam Business Association in Malaysia and Mr. Tran Van Doanh - Director of Asia Business Research and Development Center signed a Memorandum of Understanding on the implementation of Halal certification in the market Vietnam

 

The 7th ASEAN - Vietnam Economic Cooperation Forum and the ASEAN 2023 Strong Brand Announcement Ceremony, held alternately annually in Southeast Asian countries, is an opportunity for Vietnamese and ASEAN businesses to exchange and cooperate with each other. Connecting and creating momentum to promote businesses to continue to develop, raise brand value through quality products and services, gain the trust of the partners, investors, market consumers and affirm the position of Vietnam's national brand in ASEAN region.

The ASEAN - Vietnam Economic Cooperation Forum and the ASEAN 2023 Strong Brand Announcement Ceremony are held annually by the Asian Business Development Research Center in collaboration with prestigious international organizations in Southeast Asian countries (in 2017 it was held in Singapore; in 2018 it was held in Myanmar; and from 2019-2022 it was held in Vietnam). At the event, the organizer awarded 15 enterprises that have won top products and services, awarded 20 successful entrepreneurs, and awarded more than 80 enterprises with ASEAN strong brands, in which Kien Vuong Co., Ltd. was honored to receive the award of Top 10 Brands. strong ASEAN 2023.

 

Ms. Nguyen Thi Thu Hien - Vice Chair of the Board of Members of Kien Vuong Co., Ltd. receiving the award from Mr. Tran Van Doanh - Director of Asia Business Development Research Center (on the right) and Dr. Bui Van Quyen, Vice President and General Secretary of the Vietnam Invention Association, Former Director of the Southern Department of Work, Ministry of Science and Technology (on the left)

 

The criteria to choose candidate include: 1) The size of each enterprise 2) The reputation & the operation time of the enterprise 3) The brand value 4) and the Sustainable growth and development

Asia Business Centre is an applied science organization which is under the Scientific Research Association Central about Southeast Asia & Vietnam. According to practice, the Center can establish the subordinate units in the domestic ones and abroad based on the law regulations. The Centre is organized and operated based on the establishment decision No. 19-2018 / QD-TW of the Central Association and the Certificate of registration of scientific and technological activities: A-1912 of the Ministry of Science and Technology.

The event was organized and ended successfully in the evening that same night.
