Kien Vuong Company Limited ranked 330th among Vietnam’s top 500 largest enterprises in 2025

17/03/2026

On November 12, 2025, Vietnam Report Joint Stock Company (Vietnam Report), in collaboration with Vietnamnet, officially announced the VNR500 rankings. This marks the 19th consecutive year the list has been published to recognize and honor "pillar" enterprises that have made significant contributions to the development of the national economy. The rankings evaluate the overall strength of an enterprise based on objective and independent research and assessment, adhering to international standards. Key evaluation metrics include: total revenue, business efficiency (total assets, workforce size, growth rate, and profitability), and corporate media reputation. 

In 2025, Kien Vuong Company Limited rose from 337th place (in 2024) to 330th place. The announcement and awards ceremony took place on the afternoon of January 8, 2026, at the Ho Guom Opera House in Hanoi. The event was attended by over 700 guests, including government leaders, representatives from provincial and municipal People's Committees, corporate executives, and domestic and international economic experts

Representative from Kien Vuong Company Limited attended the ceremony to receive the certificate for the Top 500 Largest Enterprises in Vietnam for 2025.
