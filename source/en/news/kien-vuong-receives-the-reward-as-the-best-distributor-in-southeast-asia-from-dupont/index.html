Kien Vuong receives the reward as the Best Distributor in Southeast Asia from Dupont

08/02/2022

At 14:30 on December 5, at the headquarters of Kien Vuong Company, an intimate meeting and exchange between representatives of Kien Vuong company and representatives of Dupont corporation took place with the aim of sharing thoughts and experiences of the two companies. together to stand firmly in the market. Also in this meeting, Kien Vuong company is very honored to be awarded a medal by Dupont with the title: Best Distributor in Southeast Asia in the field of Titanium Dioxide. "This will be an important milestone marking the close cooperation between the two sides since 1996 until now." Mr. Nguyen Huu Qui - Chairman of Kien Vuong Company shares.

 Sharing about the relationship between Kien Vuong Company and Dupont, Mr. Kim Shee Young - Director of the Asia-Pacific region happily expresses his thoughts: "In 2014, Kien Vuong has achieved a quite impressive number of 218.3 billion in revenue. Hopefully, in 2015, Kien Vuong will continue to grow and the number will continue to increase. We're happy that both sides' strategies are on the same page."

The meeting took place in an intimate atmosphere, with a frank and open exchange, a lot of ideas for the two sides to work together well in the current fiercely competitive market situation. Along with that, Mr. Nguyen Huu Qui sincerely expresses: “What we feel during the 18 years of partnering with Dupont is the perfect cooperation. When we choose a partner, we do not have to choose as a buyer and a seller. It is choosing a friend so we can understand each other. Between the manufacturer and the distributor is a tight connection, when market is positive we share, and when market is tough, we sit together to solve, that is the value of Kien Vuong company.

Leadership of Kien Vuong Company and representatives of Dupont

Hopefully, after the meeting, the connection between Kien Vuong Company and Dupont will grow stronger and develop further.
