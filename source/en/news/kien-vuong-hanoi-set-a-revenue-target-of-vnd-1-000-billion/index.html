Kien Vuong Hanoi: Set a revenue target of VND 1,000 billion

08/02/2022

 (Vietnam Fisheries) - This is the number that the Hanoi Branch of Kien Vuong Co., Ltd. presents during the 15th Anniversary of the Branch's establishment which takes place on the morning of November 21, 2015 in Bac Ninh. Attendees include Mr. Nguyen Huu Qui, Chairman of the company; Ms. Nguyen Thi Thu Hien, Deputy Director of Sales and all staffs of the Branch.

Kien Vuong Co., Ltd., with main businesses are in chemicals, raw materials, additives, solvents, excipients for the pharmaceutical, food, aquaculture, animal veterinary industries... was established in 1991, headquartered in Vietnam. Head office in Ho Chi Minh City, Branch in Hanoi, representative offices in Da Nang, Nha Trang and Can Tho. In which, Hanoi Branch was established on November 23, 2000.

Mr. Bui Trung Dung, Hanoi Branch Manager, expresses that with great efforts, the branch's revenue has grown tremendously over the years, from VND 65 billion in the first years, to 2015, it is expected to reach VND 65 billion. 525 billion, and the plan by 2020 at the latest to raise the figure to VND 1,000 billion.


Mr. Nguyen Huu Qui, Chairman of the Board of Members and General Director of the company speaks at the ceremony

At the ceremony, the company's leadership highly appreciates the achievements that the Hanoi branch has achieved, hoping that the Branch will continue to make efforts, persevere and unite to achieve the set goals, and most importantly, must cover the market and contribute to the overall achievement to make Kien Vuong much stronger.
