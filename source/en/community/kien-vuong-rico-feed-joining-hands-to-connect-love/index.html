KIEN VUONG, RICO FEED: JOINING HANDS TO CONNECT LOVE

08/02/2022

With the same passion, we want to share love and bring to unfortunate children in remote, difficult areas a bit of joy for the New Year's Eve.

Yesterday, on December 11, Kien Vuong Company cooperated with Ricofeed company to give 260 presents to the unfortunate students at Binh An Primary School, Tran Quoc Toan Primary School and Long Giao Primary School. 

Students at Binh An Primary School

 

Representative of Kien Vuong and representative of Ricofeed giving presents to students of Tran Quoc Toan school

 

Primary School Long Giao

Receiving the presents given by Kien Vuong and Rico Feed, teachers and children are very happy and excited

 

“Cam My is a new district in Dong Nai Province. Built in 2014, for the past 2 years, the school has asked the Department of Education for funding to buy uniforms so that the children can wear decent clothing. When I first came to the school, seeing the children dressed in rags, it was terrible! Most of the children here are orphans or children whose parents have divorced, abandoned them and leaving them to live with grandparents. But the grandparents are old, how do they get money to support their grandchildren? That's why the children are left hanging!" Ms. Tuyet - Principal of Long Giao Primary School confided.

With the small and lovely gifts that both Kien Vuong and Ricofeed have prepared, hopefully, the children in this difficult areas will have a warm and joyful time during the upcoming Lunar New Year.

300 GIFTS HANDED OVER TO POOR CHILDREN IN HA TINH

With the help of Ha Tinh Provincial Youth Union, on August 26, Kien Vuong comes and hands over 300 sets of clothes, 300 plastic slippers and 3000 notebooks and sweets to poor students overcoming difficulties in 2 districts Thach Ha and Can Loc belong to Ha Tinh province. In order for timely gifts to reach children in the new school year, it is thanks to the cooperation with local authorities to search for students with difficult circumstances but have the will to study and practice well. The overriding goal is still to make sure the help goes to the right place, to the right person, and really means something to the recipients.
